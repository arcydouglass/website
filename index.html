Background

I am an artist and writer based in Portland, Oregon. My archives of land art research are housed at the Center for Art + Environment at the Nevada Art Museum. In 2012, I organized a conference on the work of Donald Judd with Peter Ballantine at the University of Oregon in Portland. I was also part of a research project with the National Gallery of Art to determine locations of Mark Rothko water colors in the Columbia River Gorge.

I am also available for lectures and tours. Please send me an email for more information.

Current Projects

I recently wrote an article for ARID: A Journal for Desert Art, Design and Ecology.
